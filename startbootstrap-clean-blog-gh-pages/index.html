An Exploration of Rap's Mythical Influences

To Begin:

Rap is a genre of music that has been deeply steeped in hardship, storytelling, and culture. Something that many may seem to miss, however, is the genre’s mythical influence. In fact, the myths of vintage and modern America have proven to be one of rap’s greatest influences available. Throughout this journey, this influence will be shown in a variety of ways that will allow us to better understand what myth and rap are as well as how myth has impacted the genre and culture as a whole.

Below you will find posts detailing a research-based journey into the mythical influences of early and modern rap, culminating in a better understanding of rap as a genre, storytelling device, and culture.
